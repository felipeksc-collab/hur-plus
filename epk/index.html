About

The Story

HUR+ is Taiwan's leading contemporary girl group, formed in 2021 in Taipei under AOA Entertainment Lab (星鏈艾歐亞). While most Taiwanese acts focus regionally, HUR+ has built its identity around an active cross-border production strategy — maintaining live partnerships with Korean labels Dept Music and Number K (the management of Young Posse), and Indonesian artist INDAHKUS.

The group's commercial breakthrough came with Pain Killer (2021), a single that — five years after release — continues to generate six-figure NTD streaming royalties annually. In 2025 alone the track produced NTD 237,474 in royalties, demonstrating catalog durability rare for any Taiwanese act of their generation.

HUR+ delivers what most Asian acts can't: tri-regional Asian reach in a single artist roster. For festival programmers and brand partners seeking a single gateway artist into the Mandopop, K-Pop, and Indonesian pop markets, HUR+ is uniquely positioned.

關於 HUR+

故事與定位

HUR+ 是台灣女子流行音樂團體，2021 年於台北出道，隸屬 AOA Entertainment Lab（星鏈艾歐亞）。團體核心特色為跨國合作 — 與韓國 Dept Music、Number K（Young Posse 經紀公司）、以及印尼歌手 INDAHKUS 維持深度合作關係。

代表作〈Pain Killer〉發行於 2021 年，過 5 年仍持續產生穩定串流收益，2025 年單曲權利金達新台幣 237,474 元，為其同期台灣藝人少見的長尾表現。

HUR+ 在亞洲流行音樂市場提供罕見的「單一藝人三區（台 / 韓 / 印尼）覆蓋」，是音樂祭主辦 / 品牌方進入跨亞太市場的策略性合作對象。
